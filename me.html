About me

My name is Timi Farkas AKA Wolfy. I'm 14 years old and I work with GFX (Graphic Effects) and VFX (Visual Effects) and occasionally with Youtube or. generally related to computers.

Who even am i?

My name is Timi "Wolfy" Farkaš and I come from Slovenia, I like photography, graphic design, playing games, skating. When I was 7 years old, I first started working with computers or technology in general. I used a computer from my older brother until I was 9, and then I got my first Acer laptop. I had it probably 4 years and then I got a desktop computer which I used to play games (roblox and minecraft at the time), and edit videos/vlogs when I was still active on YouTube. Over the years, I have been slowly upgrading my computer and currently my programming skills.

At 14 earning half of the average minimum wage in Slovenia

I have to admit that I really like using programs like Adobe Photoshop and Adobe After Effects. Due to these two programs, I also have my monthly earnings about half the minimum wage in Slovenia. However, I do various things such as logos, stream packs, editing from ordinary images to surrealism, etc .. I basically do these things in my free time and they relax and make me happy.

Camera and me

When I got the camera, I immediately used it for YouTube back then. I have a Canon EOS 1300D and a Canon EOS M50 Mark II which I have been using since 2017 (1300D) and since 2021 (M50 Mark II). The first thing I did when I got the EOS 1300D was to record vlogs for my channel, which ended great and went on until I lost the will to record for my youtube channel. Since then, given that we go to different “popular” destinations, I only use it to photograph people and sights.
